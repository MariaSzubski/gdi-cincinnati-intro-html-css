Unordered List

  • 2 packages dry yeast
  • 1 cup lukewarm milk
  • 1 tsp. sugar
  • 1 lb. margarine
  • 4.5 cups flour
  • 2 T. sugar

Ordered List

  1. Dissolve yeast in lukewarm milk, with 1 t. sugar. Whisk to activate yeast. Should bubble up.
  2. Cut margarine into flour, 2 T. sugar, and salt.
  3. Add dissolved yeast and beaten egg yolks. Mix well.
  4. Shape into ball, pat lightly with flour and wrap in wax paper.
